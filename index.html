What is Divide and Conquer?

Divide and conquer is a powerful algorithmic paradigm that solves complex problems by:

1

Divide

Break the problem into smaller subproblems

2

Conquer

Solve subproblems recursively

3

Combine

Merge solutions to solve the original problem

  • ‚úì Often leads to efficient algorithms
  • ‚úì Natural recursive structure

üîÄ Merge Sort

How it works:

  1. 1 Divide: Split array into two halves
  2. 2 Conquer: Recursively sort both halves
  3. 3 Combine: Merge sorted halves together

Time Complexity:

O(n log n) - Always stable performance!

Space Complexity:

O(n) - Requires additional memory
